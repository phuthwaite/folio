Profile

Professional designer based in London UK, with over 18 years of experience in creating digital solutions for highly-esteemed brands. A comprehensive skillset, deployed with a dynamic and versatile approach, has fostered success with clients from a range of industries including: media, entertainment, travel, gaming, communications, technology and ecommerce.

Lead/senior-level expertise, creative pragmatism, intrinsic knowledge of user-centred design principles, and mindful regard for business objectives and scope limitations, fulfil a passion for solving complex and technical design challenges.

Demonstrable success in multidisciplinary roles spanning the full life-cycle of a product or an end-to-end journey. Proficiency in performing relevant UX methodologies to conduct research and analysis, evaluate requirements, and derive hypotheses. In turn, generating key artifacts and deliverables throughout a project, such as user flows, customer/journey/site maps, hi/lo-fidelity mockups and prototypes, specifications, production-ready assets/content, reports and handoff documentation.
